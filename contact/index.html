Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I access my purchased courses?

After purchasing a course, you'll receive an email with access instructions. You can also log into your account and go to the "My Courses" section to access all your purchased content.

What file formats are available for templates?

Our templates are available in multiple formats including Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, and code formats (HTML, CSS, React, Vue.js). Each product page specifies the available formats.

How do I activate my digital membership?

Digital memberships are activated through license keys or email-linked accounts. You'll receive detailed activation instructions via email after purchase.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for courses and digital products. Digital memberships and software licenses are generally non-refundable due to their nature.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes, we offer special pricing for teams and organizations. Contact our sales team at sales@nextuptech.org for custom pricing and volume discounts.
